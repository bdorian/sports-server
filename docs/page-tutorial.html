Melee Tutorial
  1. In each Melee you’ll find players from the upcoming group of games. We provide each available player with a fantasy point projection ("Projection") for that game.

  2. Find the players who will MOST outperform their Post. This is their Melee Performance (MP).  Invest however much you want in any one or more players you expect to have the highest MPs. The melee is closed once the games start, and no further investments can be made.

  3. After the games are finished and fantasy points finalized, each player in the Melee is measured against the field (all other players invested in that Melee). Returns are directly proportional with Melee Performances (if Player A’s MP is 2 times Player B’s MP, Player A’s ROI will also be 2 times Player B’s ROI) and ensure a complete distribution of invested funds. Essentially, the higher Melee Performances produce a gain, while the lower Melee Performances result in a loss.

  4. The beauty of this platform is the ability to diversify. We encourage you to spread your financial exposure across more than just one player. Let’s say you liked both David Johnson and Antonio Brown, but you liked Johnson more, so you put $4,000 on him and $1500 on Antonio Brown. You would have returned $4987.52 (124.688% of $4000) from the Johnson play and $1122.20 (74.813% of $1500) from the Brown one. That’s a net gain of $609.72 - 11.1% in less than a day.
